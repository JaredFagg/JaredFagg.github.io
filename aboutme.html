About Me

Hi! I'm Jared, technical games designer focusing on current studies into digital design and aspirations into various technical fields such as computer science, web development and electronics. In the meantime, I make games and all sorts of creative works ranging from mesmerising 3D visuals to procedually generated textures ready for any kind of application.

Jared has completed a Diploma of Screen and Media (Animation, Gaming and Visual Effects) through Tafe Queensland and is currently working through a Bachelor of Digital Design (Games Design) offered from the University of Canberra.

I typically work under the following roles being programmer, 3D artist and games design (more focused towards development and technical design).

Values, Mission and Vision.

I value a synergized, communicative and light-hearted working environment without a reliance on stressful motivators as methods to produce creative works. This, in a sense also promotes community and collaboration which I also value in the creative industry.

My mission is to inspire a completely new generation to pursure game development just like me through the creation of impactful and meaningful games.

I vision myself being part of something big and working alongside a community of developers, designers and producers to create amazing experiences similar to the games that inspired me.
