Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my digital portfolio! My name is Jared Fagg and I am an adaptable designer, programmer and developer in the pursuit of my passions - Video Games & Web Development. More commonly known for Technical Games Design!

Currently awarded with a Bachelor of Digital Design with majors of both Digital Design and Games Design along with a Diploma of Screen & Media (Games Design). I have skills in both these areas along with affinities of overall programming, design, problem solving and technology.

Familiar with games development and design, my journey begun several years ago when I was creating game projects with the unity game engine and can confidently confirm my intermediate level of experience along with developing aesthetically pleasing websites in my free time.

Video Games

My greatest passion, being able to provide players with amazing memorable experiences that they can have fun with, admire, role-play, challenge and relax to. Fuelling this passion were experiences such as Final Fantasy, Persona, TEKKEN and Resident Evil. My ultimate goal is to replicate the enjoyment that are those experiences into my own creations and ultimately provide the experiences that certain game studios provided for me.

Digital Design

Logic based digital design is my overall passion in terms of digital design. Being able to create via code plays to both my strengths as a designer who understands user experience and a programmer who naturally understands code. Having said that, I still am familiar with many other areas of digital design. Overall, my goals with digital design is to help assist me in whatever I create in the future whether it is my own intellectual property or part of something bigger.
